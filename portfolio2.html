'Authority to Leave' parcel - auto order notes

About the client

Luxe at Play creates stylish products for families with little ones. The company is based in Australia.

Problem

They wanted an agreement checkbox that either allowed their delivery person to leave the parcel or request a signature for every order.

Solution

A custom Shopify Extension was built with all the above functionality. The checkbox is checked by default and when a client opts out they get a pop up warning message and an extra note is added to the order so that the delivery person can see that this clients would need to sign for their delivery. You can checkout the demo below. Add product to cart normally and you'll see the checkbox. Enjoy :)
