Geolocation Redirect

About the client

PB&Me was developed with this in mind. A low calorie, high protein alternative that can be consumed in a variety of ways. They have multiple Shopify stores

Problem

They wanted their website visitors from outside Canada to be redirect to their USD store. This was way before Shopify supported multi currency.

Solution

A custom Shopify Extension and App was built. The App automatically detects the location of the user and automatically redirects to the International Store if they are not in Canada. You can checkout the demo below. Enjoy :)
