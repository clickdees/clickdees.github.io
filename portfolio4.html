Instant Download

About the client

Yesterday was Real allows users to download High Res rare custom photos. They also sell merchandise.

Problem

A quick set-up: Their store is all about downloading 1000s of highres photos for FREE. Currently, with Shopify's digital downloads apps, you have to go through the cart + enter all your personal details to be able to download an image. This isn’t the experience they wanted users to have. The experience they wanted users to have is to select an image they like and immediately download the highres version - With a download button. Not an add to cart button. The download happens without them leaving the page (it’s immediate). They can use the theme’s current navigation to go back to ALL PHOTOS and continue to look for more images. The quirk: They also sell merchandise - T-shirts and Art Prints. They do cost $$$ and will need to use the cart. Ideally, The wanted to have the option to showcase both photos and merchandise on the home page without anything weird happening'

Solution

A custom Shopify App was built with all the above functions. The App allows instant downloads for images.Their Add to cart functionality would be enabled for merchandise. Enjoy :)
