Allow Shopify Carts to checkout on Amazon

About the client

Nightfox is a Laserware brand based in the United Kingdom. They manufacture and market a range of quality, affordable, digital night vision devices. Now their products are best-selling and frequently award-winning on Amazon.

Problem

They wanted their buyers based in the United States to order straight from Amazon instead of checking out on Shopify. The problem with this however is that there were no apps offering that functionality. Most, just linked to individual product pages on Amazon. This wasn't the best user experience and lead to a low AOV

Solution

A custom Shopify Application was built with all the needed functionality. An added functionality is that it doesn't just link to products on amazon but it actually loads a individuals cart on Shopify into Amazon. You can checkout the demo below. Add product to cart normally and you'll see the options. Enjoy :)
