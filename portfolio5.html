About the client

Infinity Goods is Denver's first zero waste grocery delivery service.

Problem

The client wanted the buyer to be able to add tips to the overall order. They also wanted a clean UX.

Solution

A custom Shopify extension was written. The extension enables buyer add a tip to their order on the cart page. Both fixed tips and custom tips are supported by the extension. You can checkout the demo below. Add a quantity of 5 products to cart normally and you'll see the options. Enjoy :)
